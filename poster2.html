能量與碳水化合物：身體的燃料

攝取足夠能量以應付手術壓力。選擇複合碳水化合物有助增強免疫力。
Consume adequate energy. Choose complex carbohydrates to support your immune system.

蛋白質：修復身體的基石

優質蛋白質有助於細胞組織修復、傷口癒合和維持肌肉質量。
High-quality protein is essential for tissue repair, wound healing, and maintaining muscle mass.

例如：肉類、魚、蛋、奶類及豆製品
e.g., Meat, fish, eggs, dairy, and soy products

特別準備：補充碳水化合物

A pre-surgery carbohydrate drink, as recommended by your doctor, can improve recovery.
